Welcome to the Robot IAT

You are invited to participate in a research study assessing your regard for humans and robots. Your responses will be used in our study that assesses the attitudes of humans toward robots. Your participation involves completing this survey.

You will complete three tasks: two brief questionnaires and an IAT in which you will sort words and images into categories as quickly as possible. You should be able to complete the tasks in less than 10 minutes total. When you finish, you will be asked some questions related to robots and then receive your results.

Please note:
     Participation in this survey is voluntary
     All responses are anonymous
     You must be 18 years or older to participate
     The survey will take about 10 minutes to complete

Disclaimer: Your reported result at the end of the test is one possible interpretation of your responses. However, neither the researchers nor the University of Nevada Reno make any claim for the validity of these suggested interpretations. .
